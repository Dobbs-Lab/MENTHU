MENTHU (Microhomology-mediated End joining kNockout Target Heuristic Utility) is a tool for identifying DNA targets likely to have a Predominant MMEJ Allele (PreMA) double-strand DNA break repair outcome. PreMA DNA targets are sites where the top predicted allele composes ≥ 50% of all total alleles, with ≥ 20% mutagenic efficiency.


Please see our full article, Robust activation of microhomology-mediated end joining for precision gene editing applications, for more information.
