MENTHU is still under active development. Updates to the tool may be pushed on a daily basis around 5pm US Central Time. You may therefore experience instability, disconnections, or crashes between 5pm and 5:10pm US CT. Please check the website version (upper left-hand corner) for reproducibility. All versions of MENTHU are archived on GitHub. Thanks for your patience!


MENTHU utilizes NCBI E-Utilities, and is in compliance with their data usage policies. Please see the NCBI Website and Data Usage Policies and Disclaimers for more information.


MENTHU (Microhomology-mediated End joining kNockout Target Heuristic Utility) is a tool for identifying sites in genes likely to have only 1-2 predominant microhomology-mediated end-joining (MMEJ) deletion patterns.


To submit a DNA sequence for analysis, please select "Calculate MENTHU Score."
